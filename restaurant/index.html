Samdeok Chicken

Daegu's original chicken+beer

Samdeok Chicken is a chimaek (fried chicken and beer) joint in the heart of downtown Daegu. It's known for its' late hours and cozy vintage interior featuring old storefront signs, and neon lights. Customer favorite menu items include soy glazed fried chicken, spicy fried chicken, and of course, cream draft beer. The pairing of chicken and beer is a staple in Korean cuisine.

Hours of Operation: 4PM to 3AM Sunday-Thursday, 4PM to 5AM Friday-Saturday.
